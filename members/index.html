Scheduling Guidelines for the Event Room and Stage

The music and event space is in demand on weekday evenings, so we need to make availability fair to all.

Weekdays after 5pm

  • All scheduling is done on the Google Calendar shared by all SRCC Members.
  • All music members can reserve the stage for 1 session per week, up to 14 days in advance.
  • Reservations can be up to 3 hours maximum.
  • Additional sessions can be scheduled 24 hours in advance with no limit on frequency.
  • To further maximize availability for everyone, we recommend schediling within 2 timeslots each evening: 5pm-8pm and 8pm-11pm.
