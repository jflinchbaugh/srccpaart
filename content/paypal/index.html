Easy Payment with PayPal

We try to make paying your membership to SRCC as easy as possible. We're using PayPal to accept Credit Cards, Debit Cards, or Venmo. Contact Us, and we'll provide you with a link to pay how you want:

  • Manually: Click the payment link every month to send your membership dues.
  • Automatically: Subscribe and automatically send your dues on the same day every month.

Subscriptions

Setup a subscription, and you can automatically send your dues on the same day every month. When you want to turn it off, you can contact us or login to PayPal and cancel your subscription for yourself.

Here's the management and cancelation process you'll see in the PayPal app:

Login to your PayPal account, and click your recurring subscriptions.

You'll see a list of your subscriptions. Click your Susquehanna River Creative Conspiracy subscription.

You'll see your Scheduled payment and Completed payments. for your SRCC subscription. Click Manage.

You'll see the details of your subscription, and again, the next payment date. If you need to cancel your subscription, click Stop Paying with PayPal.

Drop us a note, click the Stop Paying with PayPal button, and you'll not see the next automatic charge.
